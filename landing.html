The Problem We're Solving

Hard to Find Events

Open mics are scattered across Facebook groups, websites, and word-of-mouth. No central place to discover what's happening near you.

Miss Your Slot

Show up too late and the signup sheet is full. Arrive too early and waste time waiting. No way to reserve your spot in advance.

Chaotic Signups

Paper signup sheets get messy, hosts lose track of who's next, and performers don't know when they'll go on stage.

No Tracking

Can't keep track of where you've performed, what you played, or how the audience responded. No performance history.

How We're Fixing It

Find Open Mics Near You

Discover all open mics in your area with detailed information about times, locations, and what to expect.

Reserve Your Slot

Sign up for specific time slots in advance. Never show up to a full signup sheet again.

Get Reminders

Receive notifications before your performance time and updates about event changes.

Track Your Journey

Keep a record of where you've performed, what you played, and how your skills are developing.

Connect with Community

Meet other performers, discover new venues, and build your local music community.

Mobile-First Design

Access everything from your phone. Perfect for checking in at venues and managing your performances on the go.
